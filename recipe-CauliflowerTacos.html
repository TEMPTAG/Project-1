Cauliflower Tacos

Cauliflower Tacos with Chipotle Romesco Sauce are a vegetarian dish featuring roasted cauliflower florets served in tortillas, topped with a smoky, tangy chipotle romesco sauce made from roasted red peppers, nuts, garlic, and spices, offering a flavorful and satisfying alternative to traditional meat tacos.

Taco Assembly Ingredients:

  • 12-16 Corn Tortillas (Street Taco Size)
  • 1 Lime
  • 1 Fresh Bunch of Cilantro
  • 1/4 Head of Red Cabbage
  • 2 Small Avocados

Roasted Cauliflower Ingredients:

  • 2 Heads of Cauliflower
  • 4 Tablespoons of Avocado Oil
  • 2 Teaspoons of Ground Cumin
  • 2 Teaspoons of Ancho Chili Powder
  • 2 Teaspoons of Smoked Paprika
  • 2 Teaspoons of Mexican Oregano
  • 1 Teaspoon Sea Salt

Chipotle Sauce Ingredients:

  • 1/4 Cup Raw Almonds
  • 1 Can of Diced Fire-Roasted Tomatoes
  • 1 Can of Chipotle Peppers in Adobo Sauce
  • 4 Garlic Cloves with Skin
  • 2 Peeled Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Lime
  • 2 Tablespoons of Avocado Oil
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Ground Cumin
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of Smoked Paprika
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of Sea Salt
  • 1 Tablespoon of Raw Honey
