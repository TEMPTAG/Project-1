Let's Start Cooking Cauliflower Taco's

Cook the Cauliflower

1. reheat oven to 400 degrees with one rack in the middle position, and one in the bottom position


2. Rinse the Cauliflower and cut into bite-sized florets


3. Toss Cauliflower in a large bowl with the Cumin, Ancho Chili Powder, Paprika, and Salt.


4. Spread the Cauliflower on a lined baking sheet and place on the bottom rack of the oven to roast for 20min check Cauliflower at 20min, if it is not golden brown and tender, roast for another 5min


5. Remove from the Oven, prepare the tacos, and serve as fresh as possible Prepare the taco components before the Cauliflower is done roasting so you can build the tacos and serve immediately


Make the Chipotle Sauce:

1. On another lined baking sheet, add the 1/4 cup of Raw Almonds and 4 Garlic Cloves with skin.n


2. Roast the Almonds and Garlic Cloves with Skin on the middle rack for 8min Check at 8min to see if the Almonds are dark and starting to split; if not continue roasting for 2-4 more minutes - do not let the Almonds burn


3. When the Almonds and Garlic Cloves with Skin are done roasting, remove them from the oven and set aside


4. Drain the can of Fire-Roasted Tomatoes and add to a blender or food processor along with the roasted Almonds, roasted Garlic Cloves (remove skin), raw Garlic Cloves, Avocado Oil, the juice from the Lime, Paprika, Cumin, Salt, Honey, and 1 can of Chipotle Peppers in Adobo sauce


5. Blend on high until very smooth


6. Chef's unpopular extra step: Put the sauce in the refrigerator so it is cool with put on top of the fresh roasted Cauliflower when the tacos are assembled - it lightly melts the sauce and brings out great flavors while introducing a temperature dynamic to hese tacos that have an incredible textual dynamic


Prepare the taco components:

1. Slice the Lime to serve wedges alongside the Tacos.


2. Half, deseed, skin and thinly slice the Avocado.


3. Rinse and very thinly slice the Red Cabbage Also very good if it is cold from the refrigerator at time of plating


4. Rinse and dice the Cilantro


5. Heat the Tortillas in a pan on the stove



Plating the Tacos:

Put the Cauliflower in a tortilla with a generous portion of the Chipotle Sauce. Sprinkle some Red Cabbage and Cilantro on top. The Avocado can be served in the taco or on the side. Serve with a Lime Wedge. Enjoy!
