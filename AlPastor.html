Let's Start Cooking Al Pastor

Prepare the Pork:

1. Pound the Pork slices so they are even thickness.


2. Place them in the refrigerator until it is time to cook.

Prepare the marinade:

1. In a pan over medium heat, lightly brown the onions and garlic in olive oil.


2. Once lightly browned, add the seeded and stemmed guajillo chiles and bay leaves. Saute for 1-2 minutes.


3. Add 1/4 of water, and the 1/4 cup of Apple Cider Vinegar.

4. Cover on low heat for 10 minutes, then turn off heat and let it cool.

5. Pour contents of the pan into the blender with the spices (Salt, Cumin, Oregano, Black Pepper, and All Spice), Chipotle Chiles, Chicken Stock, and Pineapple Juice, and blend until very smooth.


6. Pour the contents of the blender through a fine-mesh strainer into a large bowl. Push the blended marinade through the strainer until no liquid remains. Discard the solids.
