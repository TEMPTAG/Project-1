Birria Shredded Beef

Birria Shredded Beef is a Mexican dish made from beef slow-cooked in a rich, flavorful broth infused with chili peppers, tomatoes, and spices, resulting in tender, juicy meat that is often used in tacos or served with the broth as a stew.
