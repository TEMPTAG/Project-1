Let's Start Cooking Chipotle Chicken!

Marinate the chicken thighs

1. Place the Chicken Thighs in a resealable bag, or large bowl. *Chef note: poke a few holes in the Chicken Thighs with a fork for even better results


2. Place the Chicken Thighs in a resealable bag, or large bowl. *Chef note: poke a few holes in the Chicken Thighs with a fork for even better results


3. Seal the bag or the bowl and place in the refrigerator


4. Allow the meat to marinate for at least an hour *Chef note: it is best if it is allowed to marinate overnight



Cook the Chicken Thighs


1. Remove the Chicken Thighs from the refrigerator *Chef Note: Set the bowl or resealable bag out on the counter for 30-45min before cooking


2. Preheat your BBQ grill before cooking *Chef Note: the hotter the better, as getting a little char on the marinade really brings out the flavor


3. Sear the Chicken Thighs 2min per side on the hot grill


4. Cook the Chicken Thighs until they are cooked through; 165-175 degrees internal temperature *Chef note: Chicken Thighs cook rather quickly on a hot grill, so keep a close eye on them


5. Let the Chicken Thighs rest for 10 minutes after they have been cooked, then serve in your favorite tacos, burritos, or as a stand-alone meat with your meal *Chef note: the Chicken Thighs will continue to cook while they rest, so make sure you plan for that in your cooking time and temperature
