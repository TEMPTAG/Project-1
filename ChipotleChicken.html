Let's Start Cooking Chipotle Chicken

Marinate the chicken thighs:

  1. 1. Place the Chicken Thighs in a resealable bag, or large bowl.
  2. 2. Cover the Chicken Thighs in a generous amount of the marinade
  3. 3. Seal the bag or the bowl and place in the refrigerator
  4. 4. Allow the meat to marinate for at least an hour.

Cook the Chicken Thighs

  1. 1. Remove the Chicken Thighs from the refrigerator.
  2. 2. Preheat your BBQ grill before cooking.
  3. 3. Sear the Chicken Thighs 2min per side on the hot grill
  4. 4. Cook the Chicken Thighs until they are cooked through; 165-175 degrees internal temperature.
  5. 5. Let the Chicken Thighs rest for 10 minutes after they have been cooked, then serve in your favorite tacos, burritos, or as a stand-alone meat with your meal.
